Candles that
Care

What started as a curiosity about a burning candle turned into Project Light Up My Life under my brand, Candles that Care. Driven by a passion for creativity and a desire to make a difference, I taught myself the art of candle-making. Over three months, I transformed this hobby into a successful venture, surpassing $1,500 in sales. Every dollar was donated to UNICEF to support children affected by the COVID-19 pandemic. Discover how a simple fragrance sparked a journey of learning, growth, and giving back.
